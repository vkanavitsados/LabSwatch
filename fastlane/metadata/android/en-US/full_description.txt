LabSwatch is a designer-first color utility for capturing, converting, saving, and exporting color palettes from real images.

Pick a color from a photo or camera capture, inspect its values instantly, and keep your palette library local on your device. LabSwatch is built for designers, developers, illustrators, and anyone who needs reliable color handoff without a heavy workflow.

Core features:
- Pick colors from imported images or camera captures
- Convert colors between HEX, RGB, HSL, XYZ, LAB, and LCH
- Save and organize local palettes
- Copy color values quickly
- Export palettes as JSON, CSS, Flutter Dart, or basic CxF
- Generate harmony palettes from a base color

Premium unlock:
- Remove ads
- Unlock LAB and LCH cards
- Unlock Palette Maker harmonies
- Unlock basic CxF export

Privacy-first by design:
LabSwatch does not require an account and does not sync your palettes to a cloud service. Imported images, captures, and saved palettes stay local unless you explicitly share or export them.

Color note:
LabSwatch uses standard app-calculated sRGB/D65 conversions. It does not include proprietary color libraries and is not a replacement for calibrated spectrophotometer workflows in print production.

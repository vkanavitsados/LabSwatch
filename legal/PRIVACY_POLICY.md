# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-05-17
**App Name:** LabSwatch
**Developer:** VK_Automata
**Contact:** vkanavitsados@gmail.com

LabSwatch is a privacy-first, local-first utility for designers and developers. We do not require an account, we do not sync your data to the cloud, and we do not collect personal information for our own use.

## Data Storage
All palettes created within LabSwatch are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or transmit your palettes to any server.

## Camera & Photo Permissions
- **Camera:** The app requests camera permission solely to capture images when you use the in-app camera feature. Captured images are saved to your device's `Pictures/LabSwatch` or `DCIM/LabSwatch` directory.
- **Photos:** We use the system Photo Picker to allow you to import images. This means you only grant the app access to the specific images you select. The app does not request or require broad access to your entire photo library.

## Advertising & Premium
- The free version of LabSwatch uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect device identifiers or advertising data in accordance with Google's SDK behavior to serve relevant ads.
- LabSwatch offers a Premium upgrade to remove ads and unlock additional features. In-app purchases are handled securely by Google Play Billing. LabSwatch does not process or store your payment information.

## Sharing
The app allows you to share your palettes and exported files. This feature uses the native Android share sheet, meaning data is only shared with the apps you explicitly choose (e.g., Gmail, Messenger) at the moment of sharing.

## Color Tools
LabSwatch includes free contrast checking and palette harmony generation based on app-calculated sRGB values. Premium unlocks advanced color formats and exports such as LAB/LCH, Delta E comparison, and CxF export.

## Third-Party Libraries
We do not use or include any proprietary color libraries (such as Pantone or X-Rite) within LabSwatch. Color values are app-calculated from sRGB input. LAB/LCH and Delta E values use D65 by default, with an optional experimental D50 reference-white mode where shown.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app.

# Ads & Premium Policy

## Free Version
- The free version uses Google AdMob to display banner and interstitial ads.
- **Banner Ads:** Displayed adaptively at the bottom of the main screens. They do not overlay content or navigation.
- **Interstitial Ads:** Displayed only at natural transition points (e.g., after saving a palette or returning from the share sheet). They have a minimum cooldown of 5 minutes between showings.
- **Premium Reminder:** A dismissible reminder to upgrade to Premium appears after 60 seconds of active app use. If the user chooses "Continue Free", an interstitial may be shown *only* after the dialog is dismissed.
- **Prohibited Placements:** Ads will NEVER be shown during camera use, while picking a color, during permission prompts, on app launch, or on app exit.

## Premium Version
- Purchasing Premium permanently removes all advertisements from the app.
- It unlocks advanced features including LAB conversion, LCH conversion, Palette Maker (Harmonies), and Basic CxF export.
- Premium status is verified and stored locally based on the Google Play Billing entitlement.
